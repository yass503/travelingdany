About Agafay Desert

Just 30 minutes from Marrakesh, a unique desert experience awaits

The Agafay Desert is a stunning rocky desert located just outside Marrakesh, offering breathtaking landscapes that rival the Sahara. Unlike the sandy dunes of Merzouga, Agafay features a unique combination of rocky plateaus, small dunes, and dramatic vistas of the Atlas Mountains.

Our tour combines authentic Moroccan experiences with modern comforts, allowing you to immerse yourself in the beauty of the desert without traveling far from Marrakesh. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or cultural experiences, we have the perfect package for you.
